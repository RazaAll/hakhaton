Company Introduction

GFS Builders and Developers are among the leading and diversified builders, well-known for their projects across various cities in Pakistan. They demonstrate a commitment to endless success through effective business management strategies. Under the dedicated leadership of Chief Executive Officer Mr. Irfan Wahid, who has over six years of executive-level experience in the United Kingdom and the United States, the company continually improves and delivers outstanding performance in the real estate market.

Mr. Irfan Wahid is recognized for his principles and integrity, inspiring innovation in Pakistan’s real estate market, making it more accessible and convenient for all. His leadership qualities led him to be elected as the vice chairman of the Memon Supreme Council and as a trusted member of the Association of Builders and Developers (ABAD). With an MBA from the University of Washington and real estate courses from the London School of Economics (LSE)

he showcases innovative residential development through design, craftsmanship, and responsiveness to customer needs
